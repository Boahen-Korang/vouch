How escrow works

Nobody gets paid until everybody's happy.

The crypto leaves the seller's wallet the moment a trade opens — but it goes to Vouch, not the buyer. It only moves once payment is confirmed.

01

Crypto locks in escrow

Accept an offer and the seller's crypto moves into Vouch escrow instantly. It can't be spent, withdrawn, or vanish mid-trade.

02

You pay, your way

Send by bank transfer, Revolut, Wise, PayPal, or cash, then mark it sent. The seller watches it land in their own account.

03

Escrow releases

The seller confirms the money arrived and the crypto unlocks to your wallet. If anything's off, either side opens a dispute.
